ABOUT

Assembled Realities is a game design studio established in 2018 with the intent to make web-enabled games


WORK

Our first game is a Procedural Card Game inspired by the podcast Friends at the Table, Season 4: The Twilight Mirage. The working title is:Confluent Circuit

In conjunction with our games, we are working on the Browser Memory Utility, a standalone bit of code that will act like a memory card for our browser-based games. Switching devices or just browsers but don't want to sign up for an account? "Eject" your BMU and save the JSON string somewhere you can find it again. Then, paste that blob of data into your next browser to "insert" your BMU and badda bing! Your high scores are preserved for posterity!
